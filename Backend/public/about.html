About CABII

Cars And Bikes in India (CABII) is a next-generation digital automobile marketplace built to transform how India buys and sells cars and bikes. CABII is designed on the principles of trust, transparency, and simplicity, providing a secure and user-friendly platform for individuals, dealers, and businesses.

Our mission is to simplify vehicle transactions by offering verified listings, accurate vehicle information, and a seamless digital experience. Whether you are an individual seller, a professional dealer, or a buyer, CABII ensures confidence and reliability at every step.

Our Vision & Values

  • ✔ Transparent & verified vehicle listings
  • ✔ Fair pricing and genuine sellers
  • ✔ Digital-first, user-friendly platform
  • ✔ Trust, safety, and reliability
  • ✔ Empowering buyers & sellers across India

Founder’s Introduction

MK

Mithun U. Kadam is the Founder of Cars And Bikes in India – CABII. With a strong vision to modernize India’s used vehicle ecosystem, he founded Cars And Bikes In India-CABII to bring transparency, technology, and trust into automobile buying and selling.

His focus is on building a reliable digital platform that benefits both customers and dealers, reducing fraud, and ensuring genuine transactions.
